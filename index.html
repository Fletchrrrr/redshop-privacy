RedShop Scanner — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

RedShop Scanner is a paid Android app that helps users look up trade-in prices for games, films and other media at CeX stores. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data We Collect

RedShop Scanner does not collect, store or share any personal data. All data generated by the app (scan history, purchased lists) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

Camera Permission

The app requires camera access solely to scan barcodes. Camera images are processed entirely on your device and are never stored or transmitted.

Third Party Services

RedShop Scanner connects to the following third party services to provide pricing information:

  • CeX (uk.webuy.com) — to retrieve trade-in prices for scanned items
  • PriceCharting (pricecharting.com) — to provide historic average sale prices
  • eBay — to display recent sold listings via the eBay Browse API
  • ScanDex — to identify products from barcodes
  • Open Library — to identify books from ISBNs

These services are accessed solely to provide pricing data and are subject to their own privacy policies.

Cloudflare Workers

The app uses a Cloudflare Worker as a backend proxy to securely access third party APIs. Barcode lookups are temporarily cached to improve performance. No personal data is stored in this cache.

Children's Privacy

RedShop Scanner does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
